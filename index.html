for your thoughtful consideration

to ponder life

Unlike the average author, I have not had noteworthy life experiences or an abundance of drama. My ways have always been consistent and predictable. The everyday events of my journey must seem dull to an outsider looking in. Rarely do people take an interest in what I have to share when they first meet me. This is not meant to be self-defeating or a criticism, but it is simply the truth.
Books require you to make a strong impression which I cannot do. To captivate people in a matter of mere sentences so they will invest the much larger amount of time to read further. The prospect of such a task has intimidated me and kept me from compiling my thoughts into one cohesive document. Who could make it through the mundane events which have marked my short time on this green and blue planet thus far?
So instead of trying to woo you with stories of travels to remote kingdoms or near death experiences with nature, I will work to convey the terrifying heights to which I have climbed in the imagination. How I have survived contemplating heavy topics on a regular basis and navigated deep questions with those who are interested in exploring with me.

But to those who realize the complex structure of our world, who consider the realities unseen, and marvel at the concepts unknown, I hope to give you a bit more to consider.
